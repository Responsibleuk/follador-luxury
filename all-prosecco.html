Our Prosecco

Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore DOCG
Signature Prosecco

Prosecco Superiore Follador is produced from Glera grapes, which are handpicked in vineyards located in the heart of the Conegliano Valdobbiadene area, then meticulously selected, crushed and cryomacerated by the Gianfranco Follador® method. This collection, which pays homage to the excellence of a unique terroir, comprises six DOCG: five vintages named Cru "Torri di Credazzo", Extra-Dry, Rive di Col San Martino, Brut, Extra Brut and Superiore di Cartizze.

Prosecco DOC Treviso
Prosecco DOC Treviso

These are the sparkling wines that have for time immemorial been native to this land. They are mainly produced with grapes coming from vineyards lying in the lowlands within the Prosecco area. Cold maceration is followed by secondary fermentation in autoclave tanks for approx. 60 days to produce crisp, fragrant wines suitable for all social occasions.

About Follodor

The Follador Family cares a lot about its cultural heritage in the production of wine. It has neither stood still in the past, nor is it intimidated by modern times, but has quietly married traditional cultivation techniques in the vineyards with the most modern production technologies, this to obtain wines highly regarded even by experts in the sector.
